About

Libelula - li-b√©-lu-la

Libelula translated from spanish to english is the word dragonfly. Hence, the huge dragonfly murals on the exterior of the building and throughout their awesome decor! Chef Ian started Libelula in 2019 downtown Fresno. They are located right next to the historic Crest Theater right in the heart of downtown. The best way to describe their food is farm to table but with a twist. They really strive to make their food as delicous as it can be! Libelula has won multiple awards throughout the Fresno community. You may have also seen Chef Ian on KMPH with Great Day America showing off his Art Hop specials of the day. Chef Ian likes to be innovative with his menus and even throws out some specials of the week/day. The chefs who work there always like to create a rotating menu so people get to try new foods that are in season and maybe even try something new. The menu consists anywhere from smash burgers, pizza, chilla killa burrito, curried fried chicken tacos, spicy thai mac n cheese, and the list goes on. One of the main desserts theyre known for is the BIG ASS cinnamon roll. The name surely doesnt disappoint with the roll being as big as the take out box and weighing well over 2lbs! No matter what you get there, you will be in a food coma, not only from being so full, but from the amazing food that laid on your taste buds Currently offering brunch and dinner service at our downtown location. Dinner is available Friday and Saturday 7pm-10pm. We also offer catering and large group orders.
